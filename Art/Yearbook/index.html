Yearbook
In my last two years of high school, I was part of the class that created the school yearbook. As I worked on it, I got promoted to main editor and collaborator, which allowed me to oversee the designs and layouts of spreads and even gave me the opportunity to design the "Top 5" pages for the books two years in a row.
I was tasked to pitch a theme for the yearbook and design a mock cover. I chose "movies" as my theme and recreated the Mean Girls "Burn Book" using my school colours, words, and symbols that represent my high school.
