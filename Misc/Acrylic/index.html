In 2023, I started creating keychains of my friends and family's pets. I draw the pets using Procreate and print them on heat-shrink plastic. I trace an outline around the drawing and use a hole puncher to create a hole for the jump ring. After that, I shrink the plastic and attach the hardware to create a keychain.
I used the shrink plastic to print pictures of family members for a custom rosary piece. The pictures were used in place of the pendant, and the 2nd and 5th decade beads.
Using the same plastic, I created a set of pins by printing images, shrinking them and sealing them with resin. I painted the backs to make the pins opaque and glued pin backings to them.
